Third-party libraries bundled in this extension:

- qpdf (qpdf.js, qpdf.wasm) — © Jay Berkenbilt, Apache-2.0; compiled to
  WebAssembly by @jspawn/qpdf-wasm. Strips PDF passwords losslessly (keeps the
  text layer) for normal encrypted textbooks. See qpdf-LICENSE.txt.
- pdf.js (pdf.min.js, pdf.worker.min.js) — © Mozilla, Apache-2.0 (Mozilla pdf.js
  project on GitHub). Opens iSpring's off-spec PDFs that strict tools reject;
  pages are re-rendered into a new unlocked PDF when qpdf can't.
- jsPDF (jspdf.umd.min.js) — © James Hall and contributors, MIT.
  https://github.com/parallax/jsPDF  Builds rebuilt / presentation PDFs.
- Roboto (rd-fonts.js, subset Latin+Cyrillic) — © Google, Apache-2.0. Embedded
  in jsPDF so presentation PDFs have selectable Cyrillic text.
